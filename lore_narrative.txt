# The Chronicles of the Wandering Sages

*Being an account of those who walked the world in the age before kingdoms,*
*who mapped the sacred places and gave names to the nameless,*
*whose tales became the foundation of all that would follow.*

In the time when the world was young and the boundaries between realms grew thin, 5 souls set forth from their homelands to walk the paths between places of power. They were the first cartographers of the sacred, the namers of mountains and the witnesses of wonders. These are their tales.
